Dawg Pack carnival at Husky Stadium

Are you a current member of the Husky Dawg Pack? Are you planning on joining the Dawg Pack? Do you just want some fun in the sun? Well this is the event for you!

Be sure to join us as dubs will be at the carnival for pictures and cute instagram photos. Purchase your tickets today!.
