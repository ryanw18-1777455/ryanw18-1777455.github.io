Lunch with President Cauce

President Ana Mari Cauce will host the twelfth annual President’s Picnic under a giant tent set up on the Rainier Vista lawn. Don’t miss the delicious food, music, and photos with President Cauce!

This special event takes place on May 27th on the Ranier Vista lawn. It will be a beautiful rainy Seattle day full of surprises! You must RSVP to join, tickets will be sold on a first come first serve basis. Make sure you buy your tickets soon as this event always sells out quickly.

There will be delicious food and fun games provided for everyone. Some events include a photoshoot with dubs, Q&A with the present, and a student favorite pin the tail on the professor. All catering will be by our amazing Housing and Food Services dining location local point.

Be sure to come to this once in a lifetime opportunity, you wont regret it!
